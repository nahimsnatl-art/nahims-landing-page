Who We Are

The National Association of Health Information Management Students (NAHIMS) is the official student body for Health Information Management in Nigeria, uniting all students across tertiary institutions under a vision to build a vibrant, informed, and professional community.

Led by National President Olaniyi Olaoluwa, NAHIMS is the driving force behind academic excellence, professional development, and leadership growth for its members..

Our activities range from academic seminars and community outreach programs to sporting and social events, ensuring a holistic student experience.
