About Us

Knowledge, Service, and Integrity.

Mission Statement

To make friendly environment and dymamic personalities from the members of the association; to encourage the best from diligent endeavour through the spirit of harmony, proficiency and leadership.
Furthermore, we seek to make members committed to their academics for an enriched professional ethics and standard.

Our Objectives

To encourage the spirit of oneness, friendliness, cooperation and unity among the members.

To enrich the academics, leadership development and vocational life of members.

To portray and protect member's interest and the respectable image of the association anywhere, anytime.

To involve and/or participate in the national development issues especially those of medico-social importance.

To ensure that adequate and authentic Information is disseminated as at when due.
