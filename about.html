Our History and Vision

United for Excellence in Health Information Management.

Mission Statement

To advance the professional development of Health Information Management students and promote the highest standards of healthcare data integrity within the University College Hospital community and beyond.

Our Objectives

  • To serve as the primary representative body for all HIM students in UCH.
  • To foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among students and professionals.
  • To organize educational workshops, conferences, and community health initiatives.
  • To uphold ethical practices and leadership in the healthcare sector.
